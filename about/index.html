About us

CentralApps are a North East based software development agency. We specialise in the development of online systems to solve complex business problems. We work in an open, iterative and agile way - providing transparency to our clients. Quality is at the heart of everything we do - from best practices, to testing, to code quality.

Michael Peacock, Director

Our Managing Director, Michael Peacock, is an experienced software CTO and technical strategist. He has been involved in a range of innovative projects including: large-scale telemetry data collection and processing for an Electric Vehicle company, collaboration systems for government departments, social facial recognition, national news content management and customer engagement systems. With a degree in Software Engineering from the University of Durham, Michael is also a Zend Certified Engineer. Michael is keen to share his knowledge, and to that end has had 7 technical books published, acted as technical reviewer for several more, and often speaks at technical conferences and user groups.
